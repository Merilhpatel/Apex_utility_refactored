Overview

Expert Cooling Towers Services

Cooling towers are critical to plant energy efficiency and process temperature control. Apex Utility provides comprehensive cooling tower services -- from initial design and equipment selection through to maintenance, repair and performance restoration.

We work with natural draft, mechanical draft (forced and induced), counter-flow, cross-flow and hybrid cooling towers across pharmaceutical, chemical, power and heavy manufacturing sectors.

What We Offer

Detailed Services

Cooling Tower Design Consultation

Heat load calculations, water flow design, tower sizing and material selection for new installations.

Equipment Supply

All types of cooling tower systems, fills, fans, drift eliminators, distribution systems and accessories.

Installation & Commissioning

Structural installation, pipework connection, fan commissioning and performance validation.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance including fill inspection, fan and motor checks, basin cleaning and structural inspection.

Repair & Refurbishment

Fill replacement, fan blade and gearbox repair, basin lining, distribution pipe replacement and structural repairs.

Performance Optimisation

Thermal performance testing, approach temperature analysis and upgrade recommendations to restore design performance.
