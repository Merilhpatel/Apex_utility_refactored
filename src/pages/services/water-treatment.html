Overview

Expert Water Treatment Systems Services

Clean, treated water is a critical utility for every manufacturing process. Apex Utility designs, supplies and commissions water treatment systems tailored to your source water quality and process demand.

From compact RO units for high-purity process water to large raw water clarification plants and effluent treatment systems, we provide complete solutions backed by ongoing chemical and maintenance support.

What We Offer

Detailed Services

RO Plants

Design, supply and commissioning of reverse osmosis systems for high-purity process water and drinking water.

Raw Water Treatment

Coagulation, flocculation, sedimentation and filtration systems for river, canal and bore-well water.

Ground Water Treatment

Iron removal, hardness reduction and de-fluoridation systems tailored to bore-well water quality.

Waste Water Treatment

Effluent treatment plants (ETP) including primary, secondary and tertiary treatment for trade effluent compliance.

Water Softening Systems

Ion-exchange softeners and dealkalisation units protecting boilers, cooling towers and process equipment.

Water Quality Analysis

On-site testing and laboratory analysis for pH, hardness, TDS, turbidity, microbiology and trace metals.
