Overview

Expert Air Compressors Services

Compressed air is the "fourth utility" in most plants -- yet it is frequently the least efficient. Apex Utility provides end-to-end compressed air services, from compressor selection and installation through to maintenance, repair and energy efficiency improvement.

We work with screw, reciprocating, centrifugal and scroll compressors from all major manufacturers, and provide independent advice focused on reliability and energy efficiency rather than any single brand.

What We Offer

Detailed Services

Compressor Selection & Sizing

Demand analysis, compressor type selection, pressure and flow sizing for new installations.

Installation & Commissioning

Foundation design, pipework sizing, installation supervision, commissioning and acceptance testing.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled oil, filter, separator, belt and valve maintenance to maximise compressor uptime.

Breakdown Repairs

Rapid response to compressor breakdowns -- valve repair, screw element overhaul, motor replacement.

Energy Audits

Compressed air system surveys measuring actual demand, identifying leaks and system inefficiencies.

Leak Detection & Repair

Ultrasonic leak detection surveys and repair -- typically reducing compressed air energy waste by 20-30%.
