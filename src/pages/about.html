Who We Are

Built on Engineering Expertise. Trusted by Industry.

Apex Industrial Utility Consultancy and Services was founded with a singular purpose: to deliver reliable engineering expertise and practical utility solutions that help industries improve efficiency, reduce operational costs, and achieve long-term performance.

We specialize in boilers, water treatment systems, cooling towers, air compressors, industrial chemicals and material supply, utility consultancy, plant installation, commissioning, preventive maintenance, Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), technical assistance, and skilled technical manpower supply, providing complete end-to-end industrial utility solutions under one roof.

Based in Gujarat, we provide services to pharmaceutical, chemical, food & beverage, textile, dyeing & pigment, and manufacturing industries across Gujarat by delivering engineering consultancy, utility audits, maintenance services, AMC support, technical labour supply, shutdown assistance, operational guidance, and reliable chemical & material procurement tailored to every client's needs.
